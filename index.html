This is an untrained neural network. You will need to build up a dataset (at least 1 smiley and 1 frowny) and click train before it can accurately guess whether you draw a smiley or a frowney.
  1. Draw a smiley in the box then click :).
  2. Draw a frowny in the box then click :(. Go back to step 1 (optional).
  3. Click train and wait until training finishes.
  4. Draw a smiley or frowny, click guess, and see if the trained neural net guessed correctly.
